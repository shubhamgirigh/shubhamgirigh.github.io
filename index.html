About

About Me

I am a Ph.D. Research Scholar at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay, India, working in the Interfacial Flows and Fluid-Structure Interaction Lab under the mentorship of Prof. Rajneesh Bhardwaj, Prof. Amit Agrawal, and Prof. V. Kartik. My research focuses on Fluid-Structure Interaction (FSI) and Flow-Induced Vibration (FIV), particularly emphasizing their applications in Energy Harvesting, Energy Technology, and Bio-inspired Engineering. I am passionate about investigating the intricate dynamics of fluid-structure interactions to develop innovative solutions for sustainable energy systems. By integrating theoretical modeling, experimental studies, and advanced technology, I aim to uncover fundamental insights and contribute to the development of novel renewable energy systems and bio-inspired designs. I welcome opportunities for collaboration and discussions on research ideas in fluid dynamics, energy technology, and related fields.

Projects

Projects

Dynamics of the Flow-Induced Vibration of a Flexible Cantilever Plate and its Application in Energy Harvesting

Investigated the dynamical behavior of a flexible cantilever plate in axial flow using experimental measurements and a reduced-order model. Designed and demonstrated an efficient piezoelectric energy harvester utilizing the flow-induced vibration of a plate under ambient wind conditions.

Performance enhancement of phase change material based thermal energy storage systems

Worked with the numerical model using ANSYS Fluent to study phase change material based thermal energy storage systems.

One-way Fluid Structure Interaction based Study of Liquid Sloshing Tank

Liquid sloshing is modeled using ANSYS Fluent, while fractured tank walls are analyzed with the Abaqus simulation tool to investigate a one-way coupled FSI system.

A CFD Analysis Showing Performance Enhancement of Journal Bearing by Using Al2O3-SAE30 Nanolubricant

ANSYS Fluent is employed to analyze the behavior of journal bearings using various combinations of Al₂O₃-SAE30 nanolubricant.

Aerodynamic Design of Longitudinal Head Shape of High-Speed Train by CFD Analysis

Investigated the aerodynamic performance of different head shapes of high-speed trains using ANSYS Fluent simulation tools.

Performance Enhancement of Hybrid Solar Water Heating System by CNT-Water Nanofluid

Studied the heating performance of a hybrid solar water system numerically using ANSYS Fluent and validated the numerical model through experimental testing.
